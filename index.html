Frequently Asked Questions

Here, we address common inquiries about our services, products, and policies to provide clarity and support to our valued customers. Whether you're seeking information about prescriptions, our pharmacy's operating hours, or details on our health services, you'll find the answers you need right here. Browse through our FAQ to discover helpful insights and solutions to your queries.

  • What are your pharmacy's operating hours?

    Our pharmacy is open Monday through Friday from 9:00 AM to 9:00 PM, and on Saturdays from 9:00 AM to 1:00 PM. We are closed on Sundays and major holidays.

  • How can I refill my prescription?

    You can refill your prescription in three easy ways: through our online prescription refill portal on our website, by calling our pharmacy and speaking with one of our staff members, or by visiting us in person and providing your prescription details at the counter.

  • Do you offer delivery services for medications?

    Yes, we offer free delivery services for medications to customers within a 5-mile radius of our pharmacy. Simply contact us to arrange delivery, and our team will ensure your medications are delivered safely and promptly to your doorstep.

  • Can I transfer my prescription from another pharmacy to yours?

    Absolutely! Transferring your prescription to our pharmacy is simple. Just provide us with the details of your current prescription and the name and location of the pharmacy where it is currently filled, and we'll take care of the rest.

  • Do you offer immunization services, and which vaccines are available?

    Yes, we provide immunization services for a variety of vaccines, including influenza (flu), pneumonia, shingles, and more. Our trained pharmacists can administer vaccines on-site, and we also offer information on recommended vaccines for different age groups and health conditions.
